THE FIGHTER

The main actors in Fighter 2024 includes; Hrithik Roshan , Deepika Padukone and Anil Kapoor

The film follows a terrorist organization led by Azhar AKHTAR planning to attack India,targeting the Srinagar Air Force station base of the Indian Air Force.
